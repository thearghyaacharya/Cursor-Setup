# LinkedIn Voice Notes Are the Most Underused Outreach Tool Right Now

**Author:** Vin Matano  
**Date:** 2025-03-05  
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/vinmatano/  
**Engagement:** High — emerging channel insight

## Post Content

LinkedIn voice notes have a near 100% listen rate.

Nobody is using them. That's exactly why they work.

Here's the format that gets replies:

Length: 25-35 seconds maximum.
Tone: Casual — like you're leaving a message for a colleague.

Script structure:
"Hey [Name], Vin here. 
Saw your post about [specific topic] last week — 
really agreed with your point on [specific detail].
We've been helping [similar companies] with exactly that.
Thought it might be worth a quick chat.
Let me know if you're open to it — 
happy to keep it short."

Why it works:
1. It's human — they hear your voice
2. It's specific — you referenced real content
3. It's rare — almost nobody does this
4. It's short — easy to listen to in full

Use it as touch 3 or 4 in your sequence.
After email and LinkedIn DM haven't landed a reply.
It breaks the pattern and almost always gets a response —
even if the response is "not right now."

## Key Insight

LinkedIn voice notes work because they're rare and human — 
two qualities that are increasingly scarce in an AI-saturated 
outreach environment. Used as touch 3-4, they break patterns 
that text-based messages can't.
