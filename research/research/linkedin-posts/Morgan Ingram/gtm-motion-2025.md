## Post Content

The GTM outbound motion that's winning in 2025:

Step 1: Content-led awareness
Your SDRs post on LinkedIn 3x per week.
Not company content. Personal perspectives on ICP pain points.
Prospects see your name before you ever reach out.

Step 2: Intent-triggered outreach
When a prospect engages with your content — 
likes, comments, shares — they go into a priority sequence.
They've self-selected. Treat them accordingly.

Step 3: Hyper-personalized first touch
Reference their engagement.
"Saw you engaged with my post on [topic] — 
figured it was worth reaching out directly."
That's not cold. That's context.

Step 4: Multi-channel follow-up
Standard 7-touch sequence across email, LinkedIn, phone.
But the foundation is already warm.

Step 5: Community as the long game
SDRs who build a LinkedIn following of 2,000+ ICP followers
stop doing outbound within 18 months.
Inbound finds them.

The modern SDR is a mini-media company.
Content creates pipeline. Outbound closes it.

## Why This Was Selected

Describes the emerging GTM motion where content and outbound

## Key Insight

The most efficient modern outbound motion starts with 
content — SDRs who build LinkedIn audiences convert 
their outreach at dramatically higher rates because 
prospects already know their name and trust their perspective 
before the first message arrives.
