## Post Content

Most B2B email lists have a problem nobody talks about:

30-50% of contacts return as "catch-all" emails.

Catch-all means: the domain accepts all emails regardless 
of whether the mailbox exists.

Standard email verification tools mark these as "risky" and tell you to skip them.

So most people throw them away.

That's a mistake.

With the right validation tool (we built Scrubby for exactly this), 
you can recover 40-60% of those catch-all emails as sendable.

That means a list of 10,000 contacts that standard tools cut to 6,000 
can actually be 8,000-9,000 valid contacts.

Don't leave pipeline on the table because your verification tool 
doesn't go deep enough.
