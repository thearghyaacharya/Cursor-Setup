## Post Content

Most people skip domain warm-up and then wonder why their emails land in spam.

Here's the exact system:

Week 1-2 (Warm-up phase):
- Send 5-10 emails/day per inbox
- Use a warm-up tool (Instantly, Mailreach, or Lemwarm)
- Warm-up tools send fake emails between real inboxes to build reputation
- Do not send any real outreach during this phase

Week 3-4 (Ramp phase):
- Increase to 20-30 emails/day
- Start real outreach at low volume
- Monitor bounce rate — keep below 3%
- Monitor spam complaint rate — keep below 0.1%

Week 5+ (Full send):
- 30-50 emails/day per inbox
- Rotate across minimum 3 inboxes per campaign
- Keep warm-up running in the background permanently

One domain = one inbox = 30-50 emails/day max.
To send 300 emails/day, you need 6-10 warmed inboxes minimum.
