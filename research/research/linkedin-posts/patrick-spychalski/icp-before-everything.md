## Post Content

90% of cold outreach problems are ICP problems in disguise.

Low reply rates? Wrong ICP.
High unsubscribe rates? Wrong ICP.
Meetings that don't convert? Wrong ICP.

Most teams define ICP as:
- Company size: 50-500 employees
- Industry: SaaS, Tech, Finance
- Title: VP Sales, Head of Marketing

That's not an ICP. That's a demographic filter.

A real ICP definition includes:
1. The specific pain they are actively experiencing right now
2. The trigger that makes them ready to buy (not just aware)
3. The internal champion who will push the deal through
4. The objection they will raise first — and why
5. The proof point that kills that objection

When you know those five things,
your copy writes itself.
Your targeting sharpens automatically.
Your reply rate goes up without changing a single tool.

Do the ICP work first. Everything else is downstream.

## Why This Was Selected

Reframes ICP definition from a demographic exercise to a 
behavioral and psychological one. The five-point ICP framework 
is immediately applicable to any outbound campaign and changes 
how copy, targeting, and sequencing are built.

## Key Insight

A real ICP definition includes the specific trigger that makes 
a prospect ready to buy right now — not just their company size 
and title. That trigger is what your outreach should reference.
