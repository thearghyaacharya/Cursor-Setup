## Post Content

Most people using Clay are doing data enrichment.
The best teams are using it to write copy.

Here's the exact workflow:

Step 1 — Build your list in Apollo or Sales Navigator
Export 500 targeted contacts as CSV.

Step 2 — Enrich in Clay
Pull in: company news, recent LinkedIn posts, 
job postings, tech stack, funding data.

Step 3 — Add an AI column
Prompt: "Write a 1-sentence cold email opener for [Name] 
at [Company] referencing [most recent trigger]. 
Make it sound like a curious human, not a salesperson."

Step 4 — Human review pass
Don't send AI output directly.
One person reviews 500 lines in 45 minutes.
They approve, edit, or flag for manual rewrite.

Step 5 — Push to Instantly or Smartlead
Map the AI first line to the {{personalized_line}} variable
in your sequence template.

Result: 500 genuinely personalized emails in under 2 hours.
Not mail merge. Not variable substitution. Actual personalization.

The infrastructure exists. Most teams just haven't connected the pipes.

## Why This Was Selected

End-to-end Clay workflow showing exactly how top agencies

## Key Insight

AI-powered personalization at scale requires a human review 
layer — the AI drafts, a human approves. Removing that step 
is where most teams produce outreach that feels robotic.
