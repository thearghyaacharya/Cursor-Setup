## Post Content

Most people using AI for cold email personalization are doing it wrong.

They're generating first lines like:
"I saw you went to University of Michigan — go Blue!"

That's not personalization. That's data substitution.

Real AI personalization means:
1. Pull a meaningful trigger (funding round, job posting, recent content)
2. Connect that trigger directly to your value prop
3. Make the connection feel like a human noticed it — not a script

Example of bad AI personalization:
"Hi [Name], I noticed you work at [Company]..."

Example of good AI personalization:
"Saw [Company] just opened 3 SDR roles on LinkedIn — 
usually means pipeline targets went up. 
Worth a conversation on how we help teams hit those numbers faster?"

The trigger is specific. The connection is logical. The ask is relevant.
That's the difference.
