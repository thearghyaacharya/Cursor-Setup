## Post Content

Seeing a new cold email pattern emerge: "The Mouse Trap"

Painfully short: 1-2 sentences  
Drives an impulsive reply based on priority and curiosity

The framework is simple:
1. Personalized Trigger
2. Value Prop Question

Example:
"Will, looks like you're hiring reps. Would it be helpful to get a more granular look at how they're ramping on email?"

---

## Why This Was Selected

Demonstrates a data-backed copy pattern from someone who has analyzed billions of sales emails — short, trigger-based openers that create curiosity gaps rather than pitching upfront.

## Key Insight

The best cold email opener isn't a pitch — it's a personalized trigger followed by a single yes/no question that the prospect can't ignore.
