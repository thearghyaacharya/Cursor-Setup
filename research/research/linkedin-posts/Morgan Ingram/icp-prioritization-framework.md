## Post Content

Most outbound teams treat their ICP list like a flat file.
Everyone gets the same sequence. Same cadence. Same copy.

The best teams build a tiered ICP model:

Tier 1 — Dream accounts (top 50)
- Highest ACV potential
- Perfect fit on every dimension
- Deserve fully custom, account-based outreach
- Multiple stakeholders touched simultaneously
- Budget for personalized video, direct mail, events

Tier 2 — Strong fit (next 200)
- High ACV, strong fit
- Semi-customized outreach
- AI-drafted personalization with human review
- Standard multi-touch sequence

Tier 3 — Good fit (remaining ICP)
- Average ACV, good fit
- Templated outreach with variable personalization
- Standard sequence, higher volume

The mistake: treating Tier 1 accounts like Tier 3.
The other mistake: spending Tier 1 effort on Tier 3 accounts.

Effort should be proportional to potential.
Match your investment to the return.

---

## Why This Was Selected

Introduces a tiered ICP prioritization model that directly 
determines how outreach resources should be allocated. 
Prevents the most common outbound waste: equal effort 
across unequal opportunity.

## Key Insight

Outbound ROI is maximized when effort is proportional to 
account potential. Dream accounts deserve fully custom, 
account-based outreach — not the same sequence you send to 
everyone else.
