## Post Content

The best cold email isn't cold.

Here's how the top 1% of SDRs warm prospects before the first touch:

2 weeks before outreach:
- Follow the prospect on LinkedIn
- Like 1-2 of their recent posts (genuinely relevant ones)
- Leave a thoughtful comment on a post 
  (not "Great post!" — add a real perspective)

1 week before outreach:
- Engage with their company page content
- Share one of their articles with a genuine comment added

Day of first outreach:
- Reference the engagement in your first line
"I've been following your content on [topic] — 
your take on [specific post] was one of the sharper 
perspectives I've seen on it."

What this does:
1. They recognize your name before you reach out
2. They've seen you add value in their comments
3. Your outreach arrives with context, not cold

Reply rates from this approach: 
Consistently 2-3x higher than cold-first sequences.

The investment: 10-15 minutes per prospect.
The return: a warm conversation instead of a cold pitch.
