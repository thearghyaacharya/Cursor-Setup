## Post Content

Cold email in 12 months:

1. AI writes first drafts — humans edit and approve
2. Trigger-based sending replaces scheduled sequences
   (email sends when a prospect posts, gets funded, hires, etc.)
3. Deliverability becomes the primary competitive advantage —
   everyone has AI copy, not everyone has clean infrastructure
4. List quality beats list size — 500 perfectly targeted contacts 
   outperform 5,000 generic ones
5. Multi-channel sequences become the norm —
   email alone won't be enough

The teams that win aren't the ones with the best AI prompts.
They're the ones with the best data, the cleanest infrastructure,
and the most relevant triggers.

Copy is commoditized. Systems are the moat.

## Why This Was Selected

Provides a strategic frame for where the entire cold outreach space 
is heading — essential context for building a playbook that stays 
relevant beyond 2025. The "copy is commoditized, systems are the moat" 
framing is one of the sharpest summaries of the current competitive 
landscape in outbound.

## Key Insight

As AI makes good copy universally accessible, the new differentiator 
in cold outreach is infrastructure quality, data accuracy, and 
trigger-based relevance — not writing skill alone.
