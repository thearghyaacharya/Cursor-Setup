## Post Content

Cold emailing from personal Gmail addresses lands you in spam.

Here's why:

Gmail personal accounts weren't built for bulk sending.
The moment you send 50+ emails a day from @gmail.com,
inbox providers flag your account as suspicious.

What to do instead:
1. Buy a separate domain (not your main company domain)
2. Set up SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication
3. Warm it up for 2-3 weeks before sending
4. Cap sends at 30-50 emails/day per inbox
5. Use inbox rotation across multiple warmed domains

Your primary domain's reputation is an asset.
Never risk it on cold outreach.
