## Post Content

Google and Microsoft have been cracking down all year on cold email sequences.

Updated guidance based on conversations with deliverability experts:

1. Shorter sequences — 2-3 emails. Steps 4 and 5 are proving higher risk for spam flags.
2. Softer (not weaker) tones:
   - Remove: "Sorry for being a pest"
   - Remove: "Just following up"
   - Use instead: "Am I off in thinking this is a focus?"
3. Quality matters — think of getting replies like paying off credit card debt. Banks (inbox providers) are cracking down on sub-10% repayment rates.

## Why This Was Selected

Directly actionable for anyone building outreach sequences in 2025 — the old "6-step cadence" playbook is now a deliverability liability. Based on real platform data, not opinion.

## Key Insight

Sequence length is now a deliverability variable, not just a sales variable. 2-3 email sequences with higher reply rates protect sender reputation better than longer sequences with low engagement.
